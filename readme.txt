Aggregate and embed your social media posts on your site (Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, Tumblr, Pinterest and many more) as a beautiful social media feed.

== Description ==
Social Media Aggregator Social Blend allows website owners and online marketers to create engaging feeds of hashtag campaigns and social media posts.

Social Blend will automatically fetch new posts from the various social media sources associated with each feed.

A simple shortcode can be used to embed the feed on any number of pages of your WordPress site.

To use this plugin you must create an account on [https://socialblend.com](https://socialblend.com). Then you must create a feed and add social media sources to it.

= Features =

* A beautiful social media feed of your social media content from all popular social media networks: Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Google Plus, Tumblr, Pinterest, Tumblr, Vine, RSS, Spotify, Slack, Flickr, Vimeo, Hashtags, Yelp and DeviantArt.
* Fetch your content by @username or #hashtag
* Setup rules & filters to automatically eliminate unwanted content retweets and duplicate posts.
* Moderate and remove your social media content with one click.
* Permalinks to each of your social media posts, optimized for SEO.
* Responsive grid for dynamic layouts.
* Infinite scroll: load more content simply by scrolling to the bottom of the page.
* Auto-approve all your user generated content, or set up auto-moderation filters.
* Custom CSS: Style it to fit your brand or site.
* Advanced Analytics: Find out who uses your social feed, and how they interact with it.

Visit [https://socialblend.com](https://socialblend.com) for more information and to create a free account.

== Installation ==
Download the plugin zip file, unzip it, and upload it to your wordpress site under the /wp-includes/plugins directory.

Sign up via [https://socialblend.com](https://socialblend.com) and create a feed for free

One you've done that you can use the shortcode: `[socialblend id='FEED_ID']` in any post or page

To get your Social Blend feed id:

- Sign into your account on https://socialblend.com
- Navigate to https://socialblend.com/dashboard/feeds
- Click "Edit" on the feed you wish to embed
- From the left navigation choose "Share"
- From the share type choose "WordPress Plugin"
- Copy the embed shortcode
- On the same Share options additional parameters can be set per instance of the embedded feed, for example posts per page

Alternately, you can add the feed to your theme templates directly if you like using PHP. 
Examples: 
With options as per the feed configuration on https://socialblend.com
`<?php socialblend_feed('id=feedid'); ?>`

With additional parameter to override the feed configuration posts per page to 10 posts
`<?php socialblend_feed('id=feedid&page_size=10'); ?>`
